Section 302: punishment of qatal I amd

Punished with death as qisas

Punished with death or imprisonment for life as tazir after considering the circumstances of the case and if conditions mentioned in section 304 is not fulfilled.

Punished with imprisonment for 25 years if qisas is not applicable

Clause c will not apply where principle of fasad fil ard is applied.

To constitute an offence of qatal I amd, it is not necessary that intention should be caused with intention to cause death, it is sufficient that it will likely to cause death or with the knowledge that it is so dangerous that it will likely to cause death.

The courts decide whether the offence falls in category a,b or c

Qisas punishment will apply when requirements of tazkiyah tul shahood and requirement made in section 304 is fulfilled. Otherwise, tazir punishment will be inflicted under section 302(a) 

Provisions of s.306,307,308 would only be attracted in the cases of qatal e amd liable to qisas u/s 302 (a)

Clause(b) will be applicable in mitigating circumstances where no designed pre-planned, no motive, no previous enmity, is established. Moreover, grave and sudden provocation and qatal committed on account of ghairat are also included in mitigating circumstances.

In plea of right of self defence, burden of proof is on accused.

Abettor is not liable to death as qisas, but liable to punishment of death in tazir.

Cases covered by clause(c), are:

Cases falling within ambit of section 306 punisgable under section 308

Cases neither falling in clause a or clase b 

Cases involving family honor
